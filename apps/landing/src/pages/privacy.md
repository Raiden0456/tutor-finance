## 1. What we collect

When you create an account, we store:

- Your email address and a hashed password (via [Better Auth](https://better-auth.com))
- Application data you create yourself: students, lessons, transactions, settings

We don't collect tracking analytics or build advertising profiles.

## 2. Where data is stored

Application data is stored in a PostgreSQL database. If you **self-host** Uchetka, the data lives on your own server and we never see it.

For the **hosted beta**, data is stored on infrastructure controlled by the project owner.

## 3. Third parties

The landing page loads the Onest typeface from Google Fonts. Google may receive your IP address when the font file is fetched. We don't use third-party analytics or trackers.

## 4. Cookies

We use a session cookie for authentication and a preference cookie for the light/dark theme. No tracking cookies.

## 5. Your rights

You can request export or deletion of your account and data at any time by contacting us. We will respond within a reasonable time.

## 6. Children

Uchetka is not intended for use by children under 16.

## 7. Changes

This policy may change. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
